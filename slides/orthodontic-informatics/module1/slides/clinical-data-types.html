Clinical Data Types

  • Raw Data: Data that is collected from the patient and stored unalterated.
  • Derived Data: Data that has been enahnced for easier interpretation.
  • Clinical Work Product: intellectual, analytical, and manual output created by clinicians during their interaction with patient data.
  • Raw data: like the recordings of a musician playing music. The musician owns its rights.
  • Derived data: the recording with some enhancements, like equalization, bass boost, etc. Still the musician's rights.
  • Clinical work product: you write an essay analyzing the music. Then that essay is your intellectual property.
