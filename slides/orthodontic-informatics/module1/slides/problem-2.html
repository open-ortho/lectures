Problem 2

Work Ownership

  • Software should facilitate our work by processing data.
  • Sometimes possible to export raw, unprocessed data.
  • Almost never possible to transfer processed data (our work).
Most software will let you import or export patient data like photographs or radiographs, in some cases even in DICOM. However, orthodontic software will typically not let you export the work you did with their software (like anatomical landmark selection, image type identification, clinical entries, treatment plans, ...) using a standard machine readable way in such a way to let you continue your work from another software. They might let you export in some non-modifiable format, like PDF, composite images or screenshots. Do you see the difference?
