Own the Archive (Start Small)

  • Start with a small PACS/VNA: Orthanc + Horos/Weasis.
  • Many imaging systems already support DICOM. Ask to enable it.
  • If it can push to Horos, it can push to a PACS.
  • Ask if Horos/Weasis/MicroDicom can push back into their system.
  • Never discard DICOM by converting to JPEG/TIF.
  • Common mistake: people export to JPEG/TIF because they do not know how to handle DICOM.
  • That throws away metadata and turns a clinical record into a screenshot.
  • Always keep the original DICOM in your archive. If you need a JPEG, make it a copy, not a replacement.
