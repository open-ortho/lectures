Evaluation Framework

  • Portability: export formats, completeness, time/cost
  • Interoperability: standards + evidence + bidirectional
  • Resilience: downtime mode, audit trail, identity matching
  • Total cost: interfaces, imaging storage, migrations, training

Portability Completeness

Completeness in data portability ensures that all relevant orthodontic data is exported without gaps. This includes:

  • Full patient demographics and medical history
  • Complete treatment plans and progress notes
  • All imaging data (X-rays, scans, photos) with metadata
  • Historical audit trails and version changes
  • Associated metadata (timestamps, user info, annotations)

Incomplete exports can cause clinical risks during system migrations or data sharing, potentially losing critical information needed for patient care continuity.
