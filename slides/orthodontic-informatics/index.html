Orthodontic Informatics

Data, Imaging, and Standards in Orthodontic Practice

This course covers the fundamentals of orthodontic informatics and digital workflows. The course is structured in four modules, and is intended to be accompanied by video and distributed as a MOOC (Massive Open Online Course).

Module Description
Module 1 Data Sharing, Integration, and Transition in Orthodontic Informatics
Module 2 Overcoming Challenges in Orthodontic Software Interoperability
Module 3 Global Efforts in Healthcare Interoperability – Where Does Orthodontics Stand?
Module 4 Driving Change in Orthodontic Informatics—Legislation and Community Engagement
