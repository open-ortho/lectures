openEHR

  • openehr.org: platform specs + clinical models (archetypes/templates)
  • Clinical Knowledge Manager: CKM
  • Tooling: tools and products and Modelling Tools
  • The modeling tools are the key developer experience: clinicians can define content, developers implement the platform.
  • Archetype: a reusable, versioned clinical content model (constraints + semantics) for one concept (eg, Blood Pressure, Medication Order, Problem).
  • Archetypes constrain the underlying openEHR reference model and define fields, types, allowed values, units, and structure.
  • Templates combine archetypes and further constrain them for a specific use case / form / workflow.
