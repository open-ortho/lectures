HL7 v3

  • Model-driven; messages expressed in XML
  • Heavier semantics than v2, less "free-form"
  • Ref: HL7 v3 product brief

<PRPA_IN201301UV02 xmlns="urn:hl7-org:v3">
  <id root="2.16.840.1.113883.3.72" extension="MSG00002"/>
  <creationTime value="20260221153000"/>
  <interactionId extension="PRPA_IN201301UV02"/>
  <controlActProcess>...</controlActProcess>
</PRPA_IN201301UV02>
  • Example is intentionally trimmed; v3 is a suite of specs built around the RIM.
  • This snippet shows a v3 interaction wrapper (an "interaction" is a typed message/event in a specific domain).
  • PRPA interactions are in the Patient Administration domain; PRPA_IN201301UV02 is commonly used in patient identity / patient registry flows.
  • The wrapper provides metadata: message id, creation time, interaction id, and then a structured payload (controlActProcess ...).
