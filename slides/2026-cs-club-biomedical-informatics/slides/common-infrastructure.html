Common Infrastructure

It starts as "just one app".

In reality: many systems, each with its own data model.

Plus devices: imaging, sensors, scanners, servers, networks.

  • Most clinical environments are a network of systems and devices, not a single app.
  • Click through: single software -> many software systems -> the underlying hardware/devices.
  • This is why standards matter: the plumbing is shared, and everyone needs to interoperate.
