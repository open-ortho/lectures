IODs and MWL

  • IOD: a static object type (CT Image, SR, Segmentation, etc.)
  • Workflow: services that coordinate work between systems
  • MWL: Modality Worklist (scheduled procedure -> modality)
  • Browse CIODs: dicom.innolitics.com/ciods
  • Spec: PS3.3 and PS3.4
An IOD is the "shape" of a DICOM dataset. MWL is one example of DICOM workflow infrastructure.
